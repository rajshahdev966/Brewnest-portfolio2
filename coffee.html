Why Our Beans?

Uncompromising Standards from Soil to Cup

Direct Trade Ethics

We work directly with local farmers in Chikmagalur and international estates, paying 50% above Fair Trade standards to ensure sustainable agrarian practices.

Precision Hot Air Roasting

Using Loring smart zero-emission roasters, we maintain heat accuracy within 0.1°C to fully unlock specific origin volatiles and caramelize sweet compounds.

Freshness Guaranteed

Your coffee is shipped within 48 hours of roasting. Degassing of carbon compounds happens in transit inside valved bags for optimal taste profile on arrival.
