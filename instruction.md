# BrewNest Café — Premium Website Wireframe & UX Strategy

## Brand Positioning

BrewNest Café is positioned as a modern specialty coffee and workspace café experience in Bengaluru. The website should feel premium, cinematic, modern, and community-driven while maintaining excellent usability and mobile responsiveness.

The digital experience should combine:

* Specialty coffee branding
* Workspace culture
* Lifestyle aesthetics
* Delivery convenience
* Modern hospitality UX

The overall experience should resemble a luxury lifestyle brand rather than a traditional café website.

# Animation Style

Animations should be:

* subtle
* smooth
* premium
* cinematic

Avoid flashy effects.

Recommended:

* soft fade reveals
* parallax scrolling
* image masking
* magnetic buttons
* smooth hover transitions
* gentle floating effects

All animations must remain mobile-optimized.
